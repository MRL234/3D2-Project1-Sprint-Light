Process: Motion


Materials and Inspiration

At first with this servo motor, I thought about creating something rotating windmill, clock, fishing, or Cat toy, Puppet, Fan, Dog's tail, Opening window or door, Fidget Spinner, Dentures, Mailbox etc. However, after watching example videos, almost any motion can be made by this servo. So walking, grabbing, moving can be made to so it would be interesting to research on that part.

What I have decided to do:

Sweeb,Knob & Sequence exercise

These are the circuit for the exercise

1) Sweeb

The motor is moving 180 degree constantly. When it reaches 180 degree both sides, it stops automatically and turns around.

2) Knob

It was amazing experience using new tool potentiometer. This is working as a controller so we can control the servo motor by moving around this.

3) Sequence

In this exercise, the servo motor is moving, changing it's angle that is set to be on Arduino. It is moving bit creacky since It has several different angles are set.
